Indiana Jones

Raiders of the Lost Ark

Lucas conceived Raiders of the the Lost Ark in the early 1970's. Seeking to mondernize the serial films of the early 20th-century, he developed the idea further with Kaufman, whi suggested the Ark as the film's goaal. Lucas eventually focused on developing his 1977 opera Star Wars.

Temple of Doom

The film was released to financial success but initial reviews were mixed criticizing its darker elements and violence. However, critical opinion has improved since 1984, citing the film's intensity and imagination.

The Last Crusade

After a mixed reaction to Indiana Jones and the Temple of Doom, Spielberg chose to tone down the dark tone and graphic violence in the next installments during the five years between the Temple of Doom and The Last Crusade, he and executive producer Lucas reviewed several scripts before accepting Jefferey Boam's.
